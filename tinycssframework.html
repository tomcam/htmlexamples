Universal web page framework

In under 10 lines of CSS

This framework is a well-formed, search engine-compliant starting point for pages that include a header at the to of the page, a nav section immediately after, an article section, and a sidebar (using the aside tag.)

While this example of the aside puts the sidebar on the right, just replace the float:left; for the article, aside rule with float:right; and replace the article: {clear:left;} with article: {clear:right;} rule to swap sidebar positions.
